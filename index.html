Diego's Bare-Metal Raspberry Pi Blog

This blog documents my journey building a full multicore operating system on the Raspberry Pi 3B, with the long-term goal of creating a fully-featured OS accompanied by comprehensive documentation for anyone interested in bare-metal programming on the Pi. My aim is to explain everything as clearly and thoroughly as I can—but there will be times when I don't yet fully understand something, or where pieces may be missing. In those cases, I’ll do my best to note that clearly.
